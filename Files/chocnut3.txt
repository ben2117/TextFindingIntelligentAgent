1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a 7cm deep, 10cm x 21cm (base) loaf pan and line base and sides with baking paper, allowing a 2cm overhang at both long ends.
2. Place flour, cocoa, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and sugar in a food processor. Process for 30 seconds or until well combined. Add egg, oil and milk. Process until smooth. Transfer to a large bowl. Stir in raisins and hazelnuts.
3. Pour mixture into pan. Bake cake for 50 to 60 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. Stand for 10 minutes before lifting onto a wire rack to cool.
4. Make icing Place chocolate in a heatproof bowl. Heat cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until hot (do not boil). Pour over chocolate and stir until smooth and well combined. Stand for 30 minutes or until a spreadable consistency.
5. Spread icing over cake. Serve.
