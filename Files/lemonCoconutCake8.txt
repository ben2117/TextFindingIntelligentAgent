Lemon & Coconut Cake

1. Preheat oven to 180C. Grease and line a 16 x 26cm (base measurement) slice pan, allowing sides to overhang.
2. Beat butter, sugar and vanilla in a bowl until pale and creamy. Beat in egg. Fold in combined flour and milk.
3. Spread evenly over base of pan. Bake for 15- 20 minutes or until a skewer inserted into centre comes out clean.
4. Spread Buttery Slice Base with 3/4 cup Yackandandah Lemon Curd.
5. Use an electric beater to whisk 2 egg whites in a clean dry bowl until soft peaks form. Whisk in 1/2 cup caster sugar, 1 tablespoon at a time. Fold in 1/2 cup desiccated coconut. Spread meringue mixture over the lemon curd. Sprinkle with 1/4 cup shredded coconut. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes. Cool in pan. Cut into pieces.
